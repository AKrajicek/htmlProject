My Passion:Golf

By:Adam Krajicek

I started playing golf in 5th grade and I have loved it ever since. I love golf because it is a very complex sport. Golf is a very mental game. You might be having a great round but one bad hole can change your whole round. I play golf for Northview's varisty team. I also work at Golf Galaxy, so my life is surrounded by golf.
